SoC and Modularity Key Points

"The value of Separation of Concerns
is simplifying development and maintenance
of computer programs.
When concerns are well separated,
individual sections can be developed
and updated independently.
Of especial value is the ability to later
improve or modify one section of code without
having to know the details of other sections,
and without having to make corresponding
changes to those sections."

	return pr;

What are
Service Layer benefits?

Decouples complexity
Allows reuse from multiple entry points
Encapsulates concern logic
Organizes where business logic resides
Supports SFDX modular development

When is a Service Layer
worth the effort?

Evolving requirements
Growing scope of your org
Expanding or distributed team
Multiple execution context clients
Building a product

What are
Service Layer guidelines?

Must be caller agnostic
Must follow bulkification and limits guidelines
Must be maintained to avoid 'Leaks'
Should follow strict naming conventions
Should handle Unit of Work for DML transactions
May be synchronous or asynchronous
